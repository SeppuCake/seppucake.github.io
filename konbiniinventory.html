About the Project

Konbini Inventory aim to enhance and introduce an efficient, robust, and reliable performance in maintaining inventory in the retail industry. Inventory management has always been the key for retail industry to keep track of their stocks and sales performance for a product. With the use of NFC technology incorporated in this system, Konbini Inventory promotes an innovative and user-friendly shopping experience to the consumers in Malaysia.
Most retail industry rely on barcode scanners which has been in use since 1974 and revolutionized retail industry with its use. However, barcode can be unreliable as it wears down due to human errors that contributes to congestion upon checkout counters in a retail store. This system aim to improve and compete with the current trend of contactless approach in purchasing and delivery. Moreover, it ensures the checkout procedure performance to improve and reduces the congestion upon checkout.
List of Modules built in this system are listed below:

  • Homepage:
    • Purpose: Displays the page to welcome users to the system with simple explanation to its purpose and encourages users of their work ethics in keeping their inventory up to date.
    • Importance: Enables users to have a sense of the system's purpose and also allows the management team to put up goals and objectives for their company.
  • Product Registration Module
    • Purpose: Facilitates the registration of NFC tags and management of products in the system, including details such as product information, pricing, and inventory.
    • Importance: Enables efficient management of the company's product catalog, streamlining processes related to product creation, updates, and availability tracking.
  • Create, Read, Update, Delete (CRUD) Modules (For Product & Procurement Modules)
    • Purpose: Provides basic CRUD functionality for managing data related to products and procurements, allowing users to create, view, update, and delete records.
    • Importance: Enables users to perform essential operations on product and procurement data, ensuring data accuracy, integrity, and efficient data management.
