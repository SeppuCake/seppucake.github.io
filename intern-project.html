About the Project

CompuRex Corporation is a company that provides Software as a Service (SaaS). Delivering new technological solutions and digital media contents to organizations. I am delighted for the opportunity to work with this company with the amount of projects and tasks each employee responsible to deliver. Through this project, I was tasked to create a responsive website and a dashboard for statistics displays such as number of orders, number of procurements, and etcetera. The project also required me to create a database included with the Entity Relationship Diagram (ERD) for each modules such as:

  • User Login Module:
    • Purpose: Provides a secure authentication system for users to log in to the website.
    • Importance: Ensures that only authorized users can access the system, protecting sensitive information and maintaining data privacy.
  • Dashboard:
    • Purpose: Displays statistics and key metrics related to the business, such as the number of orders, procurements, and other relevant data.
    • Importance: Enables users to have a centralized view of important information, facilitating data-driven decision-making and providing insights into the company's performance.
  • User Access Control Module
    • Purpose: Manages user roles and permissions, allowing administrators to control access to different parts of the system.
    • Importance: Ensures that the right level of access is granted to users based on their roles, enhancing security and preventing unauthorized access to sensitive functionalities.
  • Product Registration Module
    • Purpose: Facilitates the registration and management of products in the system, including details such as product information, pricing, and inventory.
    • Importance: Enables efficient management of the company's product catalog, streamlining processes related to product creation, updates, and availability tracking.
  • Procurement Registration Module
    • Purpose: Handles the registration and tracking of procurement activities, including supplier information, purchase orders, and delivery status.
    • Importance: Supports the procurement process by providing a centralized system for managing supplier relationships, monitoring orders, and ensuring timely delivery of goods or services.
  • Messaging Module
    • Purpose: Enables communication and collaboration within the system, allowing users to send messages, notifications, or alerts to each other.
    • Importance: Facilitates efficient communication among team members, enhancing collaboration, sharing of information, and timely decision-making.
  • Create, Read, Update, Delete (CRUD) Modules (For Product & Procurement Modules)
    • Purpose: Provides basic CRUD functionality for managing data related to products and procurements, allowing users to create, view, update, and delete records.
    • Importance: Enables users to perform essential operations on product and procurement data, ensuring data accuracy, integrity, and efficient data management.
